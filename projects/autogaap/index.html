📘 ALCDRE Rules

  • Assets – Debit to increase
  • Liabilities – Credit to increase
  • Capital – Credit to increase
  • Drawing – Debit to increase
  • Revenue – Credit to increase
  • Expense – Debit to increase

📘 Accounting Equation

Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

📘 Contra Accounts

A contra account offsets the balance of a related account:
e.g. Accumulated Depreciation offsets Equipment.
