Getting started

How to put Auto GAAP to work in minutes

Launch the sandbox with your own ledger or the provided sample file, then let AutoLedger narrate every reconciliation step. Each stage is adaptive, so finance, revenue, and engineering partners stay in sync.

1

Upload or connect your ledger

Drop in a CSV, connect an ERP source, or load the sample ledger to explore the control room.

Auto GAAP ingests structured and semi-structured journal data. Use the sample ledger to see baseline flows, or authenticate a live data source when you are ready for full automation.

2

Choose your accounting mode

Select cash or accrual handling so AutoLedger applies the right recognition cadence.

Toggle policy packs for cash or accrual workflows. The sandbox updates balances, scheduling, and disclosure prompts instantly so you can validate controls before production rollout.

3

Run the Auto GAAP analyzer

Click Analyze to surface narratives, recommendations, and trial balance views.

The analyzer inspects every entry, grouping line items by GAAP category and highlighting imbalances, stale policies, or missing evidence so you can act before close deadlines.

4

Let AutoLedger categorize transactions

Activate auto-categorization to balance the books and push updates into your ledger view.

AutoLedger monitors every journal event, applying policy graph rules, syncing recurring entries, and exporting ledger or trial balance snapshots that remain audit-ready by design.
